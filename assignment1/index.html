About Me

Gentlemen, Fair Ladies gather round. I have an introduction to make. Bursting on to the Technology scene I am here to present the first website made by Frank A. Capraro.

Who is Frank you ask? Let me give you a bit of his background.

Frank grew up in Central New Jersey with his parents and siblings. After graduating high school Frank attended Lehigh University where he received a degrees in International Relations and a graduate degree in Technical Entrepreneurship. Currently he works as a product owner and program manager for a large technology company.

When not working or attending classes Frank enjoys cooking, reading any book available and traveling. For the past 10 months Frank has traveled to a new city every month.

Thanks for reading! And remember to stay tuned for the exciting journey of Frank A. Capraro.
